About Nimmia Traditional Jewellery

At Nimmia Traditional Jewellery, we are more than just a brand — we are a tribute to India’s glorious heritage, artistry, and culture. Since our founding in 2010, our mission has been to bring timeless beauty to modern lives through intricately crafted traditional jewellery.

Our collections are inspired by the stories of royal dynasties, temple traditions, and age-old artisanal techniques that have been passed down for generations. Every piece is a work of art — carefully designed, delicately handcrafted, and made to celebrate the essence of femininity and grace.

Our Journey

2010 - The Beginning

Founded with a vision to revive traditional Indian jewellery, we started with a small team of passionate artisans.

2015 - Expanding Craft

We collaborated with artisans across Rajasthan and Tamil Nadu, expanding our styles and materials.

2018 - Online Presence

Our collections launched online, making timeless heritage accessible worldwide.
